## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### **Maximize Engagement:**
1. **Ask Questions in Polls** - Get instant feedback from your audience
2. **Use Relevant Hashtags** - Make your posts discoverable (3-5 hashtags per post)
3. **Mention People** - Tag relevant people to start conversations
4. **React Authentically** - Use reactions that match your feelings
5. **Bookmark Useful Content** - Build your personal knowledge base

### **Best Practices:**

#### For Polls:
- Keep questions short and clear
- Limit to 4 options for better participation
- Use polls for decisions, opinions, or quick surveys

#### For Reactions:
- React to show appreciation without commenting
- Use specific reactions (not just "Like") to communicate better
- Check post reactions to understand audience sentiment

#### For Hashtags:
- Use lowercase (#javascript not #JavaScript)
- Keep hashtags relevant to content
- Mix popular and niche hashtags
- Don't overuse (3-5 per post is ideal)

#### For Mentions:
- Only mention relevant people
- Use mentions to start conversations
- Thank people with mentions
- Credit collaborators in your posts

#### For Bookmarks:
- Save posts you want to reference later
- Bookmark tutorials and guides
- Organize important announcements
- Clean up old bookmarks regularly
